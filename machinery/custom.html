Parallel Jig

Setup

  

To increase throughput and gravel washing capacity. This technique provide easy maintainability and rapid scalability without much hassle.
 

External

Trommel Setup

  

Isolated trommel from the jig and operate as separate entity. This option is required when operating on parallel jig setup option and provide greater filtration when needed.
 

Gold and Black

Sand

Separation

  

Black sand separation unit

Custom built units to filter gold mixed black-sand (Alluvial Gold) from the gravel washing machine. Most of the time, these units can be configured to drive directly from the main engine.

Gold separation using carpet

Apart from the black-sand unit, this specially designed carpet can be used to further filter gold particle (Alluvial Gold) from the black-sand.
 

Construction

Sand

Separation

  

Areas with high demand for construction sand can benefit from this custom configuration with sand production.
 

Gravel Sucking

Pump

  

A gravel sucking pump can be used where backhoes/excavators are not able to reach. These pumps can pump gem/gold bearing gravel straight to the washing plant.
 

Water Splitter

Dimension

Changes

  

Depending on the available water pump we can customise the splitter to provide optimum pressure and the water flow.

About Us
